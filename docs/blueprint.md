# **App Name**: MindFlow AI

## Core Features:

- AI Chat Assistant: An interactive AI-powered chatbot that guides users, understands their mood, and assists with journaling and recommendations.
- Personalized Wellbeing Tool: A generative AI tool that provides tailored song recommendations, wellness tips, and activity suggestions based on user input and mood.
- Secure Digital Journal: A private space for users to record their daily thoughts and feelings, accessible only to them.
- Journal History View: A chronological and organized view to easily browse and revisit past journal entries.
- User Authentication & Profiles: Secure user registration, login, and personalized profiles to maintain data privacy and session persistence.

## Style Guidelines:

- Dark color scheme emphasizing introspection and calm. Background: A deep, muted slate blue (#1C1F22) for a sophisticated monochrome foundation. This subtly cool background creates a focused and tranquil digital environment, enhancing readability for text-heavy journaling.
- Primary Color: A light, serene cool gray (#D3D9DE) that stands out softly against the dark background. This color will be used for main text, UI elements, and non-interactive components, maintaining the app's clean, minimalist, and modern feel without being stark.
- Accent Color: A vibrant yet tranquil aqua (#66CCCC) for interactive elements, call-to-action buttons, and subtle highlights. This analogous hue provides an energetic focal point and clear visual cues within the monochrome design, offering good contrast against both the primary and background colors.
- Headline font: 'Space Grotesk' (sans-serif) for its modern, slightly technical, yet inviting appearance, suitable for headings and chatbot responses. Body font: 'Inter' (sans-serif) for excellent readability and neutrality, perfect for longer journal entries and wellness tips.
- Minimalist line-art or filled icons in shades of the primary and accent colors, maintaining a clean and modern aesthetic. Icons will be subtly animated to provide feedback and visual appeal without distracting.
- Clean, intuitive, and adaptive layout with generous use of dark space to enhance focus. A conversational UI paradigm for the chatbot, complemented by clearly defined sections for journaling and recommendations.
- Smooth and subtle animations for transitions between sections, fluid loading indicators for AI responses, and interactive chat bubble animations. Animations will be purposeful, enhancing the user experience and visual appeal without compromising performance.
